Work

Experienced Tutor, CRB Checked

Olga Lawrence-Jones is a professional tutor based near Canterbury, in Kent. Olga teaches both children and adults Maths and English, and prepares applicants for Common Entrance and for the Kent Test.

Olga is an accomplished teacher, with extensive teaching experience. She has taught privately for 10 years. Many of Olga's students have got into top grammar schools, including Simon Langton Boys and Girls Schools, Barton Court and Queen Elizabeth's. She specializes in working with students with low confidence, often boosting their grades from C/Ds to A/Bs.

About

Olga Lawrence-Jones | Tutor

Olga Lawrence-Jones is an experienced educator, based near Canterbury, in Kent. She is married, with three sons.

Olga studied Computer Science and Maths at the University of Central London, one of the first universities to offer the course, and gradated with a 1st Class degree. She went on to work as a programmer, systems analyst and project manager at large organisations including the Ministry of Defence and N. M. Rothschild.

After developing a strong interest in education, Olga began teaching, and has been teaching professionally for 10 years. She is also a certified Homeopath and Councellor, and believes in a holistic approach to both education and wellbeing.

In her free time, Olga enjoys reading, riding and travelling, and is a long time volunteer for Riding for the Disabled.
